Car Lease Tracker

About the App

Car Lease Tracker is your essential companion for managing and monitoring your car lease usage. Whether you're tracking business trips or personal journeys, our app helps you stay within your lease mileage limits and avoid unexpected charges.

Key Features

  • Easy odometer reading tracking
  • Special trip logging with notes
  • Real-time mileage calculations
  • Monthly usage statistics
  • Remaining mileage predictions
  • Visual progress indicators
  • Local data storage for privacy
  • Clean, intuitive interface

How It Works

Simply input your lease details including the total allowed mileage and contract duration. The app will help you track your usage and provide insights to help you stay within your limits. All data is stored locally on your device for maximum privacy and security.

Why Choose Car Lease Tracker?

  • No account required
  • No internet connection needed
  • Complete privacy - all data stays on your device
  • Regular updates and improvements
  • Designed specifically for lease tracking
